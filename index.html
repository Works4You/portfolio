About me

I'm a product and business leader who has spent 11+ years turning ideas into operating companies — as a two-time founder and as the product owner behind live technology platforms.

Today I'm co-founder of Works4you, an AI-powered B2B customer-acquisition engine, where I lead product and business development from concept through build. In parallel I run product for POKER111, a live SaaS platform serving 100+ international users with distributed engineering teams across three continents.

Before that I founded and scaled TWOTONE, a retail + e-commerce brand, to 100+ boutique partners with overseas manufacturing and full P&L ownership. I combine product thinking, AI tooling, and hard operational and financial discipline — I don't just imagine the product, I build the machine that ships it and prove it works before spending.

How I Work

My approach to product

Idea → machine

I take a raw idea and turn it into a working system — specs, architecture, tooling, and the team to ship it. Vision only counts when it runs in production.

Prove before you spend

I pressure-test the assumptions a business is built on with cheap, fast experiments — and I'll kill my own idea when the data says so, before real money is at risk.

Own the P&L

Two companies founded, one SaaS run today. I connect product decisions to unit economics, cash flow, and growth — not just features.
