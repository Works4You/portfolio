About me

I'm a product and technology leader who has spent 11+ years turning ideas into operating companies - as a founder and as the person who owns and builds the product itself.

Today I'm co-founder of Works4you, where I own the technology product - building it hands-on with AI and vibe-coding - and drive business development. Before that I ran product and operations for POKER111, a venture that scouted, tested, and sponsored aspiring Brazilian poker players in international events, where I recruited and led development teams across three continents.

I founded and led TWOTONE and its daughter brand Monochrome - a two-brand retail + e-commerce fashion house I ran end to end for a decade, from production, finance, and marketing to 100+ boutique partners and the entire business-technology stack. I don't just imagine the product; I build it and prove it works before spending.

How I Work

My approach to product

Idea → machine

I take a raw idea and turn it into a working system - specs, architecture, tooling, and the team to ship it. Vision only counts when it runs in production.

Prove before you spend

I pressure-test the assumptions a business is built on with cheap, fast experiments - and I'll kill my own idea when the data says so, before real money is at risk.

Own the P&L

Two ventures founded and a brand run end to end for a decade. I connect product decisions to unit economics, cash flow, and growth - not just features.
